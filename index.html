In its most basic form, anime refers to animation. Interestingly enough, the name itself isn't an abbreviation of the English word animation. Instead, it's how you say “animated cartoon” (written アニメ) in Japanese. To a Japanese viewer, anime is any cartoon, whether it's made in Japan or not.
